What is the Marine Corps?

The Marine Corps are responsible for keeping guard of the United States of America and its surroundings through land and water. The Marine Corps also take part with the United States NAVY in international operations all over the globle. The USMC (United States Marine Corps) has been part of the United States Department of Defense since 10 November 1775 (249 years, 3 months)
