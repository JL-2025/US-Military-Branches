What is The Army?

The Army is one of the strongest military branches in the world with over 1.5 million active personnel ranging from active soldiers to civilian forces. The Army was first founded in June 14, 1775, making it one year older than the Declaration of Independece. The Army has taken part in dozens of mutiple wars and conflics around the U.S. Some of their biggest wars and conflicts are:

  • The War of Afghanistan
  • The War of Iraq
  • World War I
  • World War II
  • The Vietman War
  • The Korean War
  • The American Civil War
  • The War of 1812
  • The Persian-Gulf War
  • The Quasi War
  • The American-Revolutionary War

How Can You Join the Army?

To enlist in the Army, there are 5 steps you must follow:

  1. Speak with a recruiter - Skpeaking to a recruiter is crucial if you want to enlist since they will help you find whats the best option or career you want to enlist for.
  2. Take an entrance test - Once you've decided what to enlist for, you will have to take the Armed Services Vocational Aptitude Battery (ASVAB) as it helps you find whats your position depending on your skills.
  3. Complete a Physical Exam - Your recruiter will help set up an appointment at a Military Entrance Processing Station (MEPS) to evaluate if your physically ready to join.
  4. Find an Army job - A career coundelor will meet up with you and help you choose your job based on skills among 200 different sorts of positions and jobs.
  5. Sign a contract and prepare for basic training - You will have to sign a contract that shows you are ready to serve in the Army and will then have to take basic training
